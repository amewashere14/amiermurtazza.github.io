ABOUT ME

I'm just a guy living a student life day by day in my university's dormatory and occasionally doodle on any medium I can get my hands on. I mostly post my sketches and illustrations online, with a bit of other projects spliced within the mix. I started creating illustrations when i was in highschool. Back then, I was influenced by dozens of artist and illustrators I followed online.

I lived a rather mundane life where I never utilised my creativity for anthing other than work. due to that starting was rather scary. However once I started my work, it was smooth sailing from there. to hone my art, i watched and studied artists and through process of trial and error, it led me to become the illustrator I am today.

"What drives me"


When I'm active online, I get motivated by seeing peoples art and illustrations so much that I want to try making something similar but unique myself. I mostly browse through platforms such as Pinterest, Instagram, Pixiv, Twitter and Tumbler in order to get inspiration.

I also spend a lot of time travelling and going out for outings with my friends and family. The time spent and the pictures taken in that trip made me compelled to tinker with the photo to make it more impactful.
